About Me

Hi, I'm Tyler! I’m a software engineer with a love for web development. I have extensive experience writing code and building full stack web applications. I have a strong track record as a technician and an educator, including my most recent experience as a resident software engineer and technical mentor at Hack Reactor, a software engineering emmersive that targets career changers and veterans.

I firmly believe that the skills necessary for success as a classical musician also heavily correlate to those that make a succesfull software engineer. Both disciplines require countless hours of study, focus, practice, tenacity, and discipline. Combined with my teaching experience, I’m excited to see how these qualities enable me to grow into an even greater software engineer. Some of my other unique leadership experiences include being elected as the secretary for the Wilco Guitar Society board of directors, and previously as the Vice President of a cooperative. I was also trained and contracted as a conflict mediator. I love language-learning and have limited working proficiency in Japanese.
